Rules

  • We get together one weekend and build something (and finish it!).
  • The source will be released under the GPL-2.0.
  • The product must be online and usable at the end of the event.

We need you to pitch us your idea for a Python project that can be finished in one weekend.

Please send it to us, and tell us this:

  • What is it?
  • What is it for?
  • What features does it have? List the features and describe them.
  • How will we organize ourselves? What features can be developed in parallel?

The final date to send us your ideas is Sunday, November 11th. This gives us at least 6 days to review it and help you get it ready.

Schedule

Saturday, November 17th
  1. 10h: Each pitcher gets 3 minutes to explain their idea.
  2. First voting.
  3. The pitchers of the 3 most voted ideas get 15 minutes to explain them in more detail.
  4. Second voting. The most voted idea is chosen!
  5. We make task forces and divide the work
  6. We hack!
  7. 19h: First demo.
Sunday, November 18th
  1. 10h: We continue hacking!
  2. 18h: Final demo.
  3. Beer!
